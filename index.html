About the author

Though living her Canadian dream, the inspirations and epiphanies for Tanya’s stories are well rooted from her homeland, India: the land of storytelling. While at daytime she can be found immersed in her excel sheets, files and meetings; the dusk is solely dedicated to her stories.

Tanya started her literary journey with ‘Dear Diary…’ followed by anthologies named ‘Crumpled Voices’,‘Innocence Lost’, ‘Strangled Emotions’ where she was a contributing author. Tanya also wrote foreword for an anthology named ‘Drenched Souls’ all under the aegis of her favourite publishing house, Gargi Publishers.

After her MBA from IMT-Nagpur and a brief corporate career in India, Tanya decided to settle down in Toronto in 2018. She had developed camaraderie with her pen when she was quite young and since then she has been a very ardent reader and writer. Poems have a special place in her collections as she believes that those rhyming couplets have a very exquisite way of expressing emotions. Tanya loves to compose her work in Hindi, Urdu as well as English and is currently learning French.

Tanya aspires to be a bestseller someday!
